Interests

"Solitude" is how I summarise my interests. To a typical introvert like me, time alone is not only a special occasion to relax after stressful work but also my unique space to contemplate and explore my own inner "world" with special values

- BOOKS -

Books have been my best friends since I started learning the very first letters. In the beginning, I only considered books as a way to entertain myself in the absence of other high-tech devices. However, gradually, books became an irreplaceable part of my day. Through my journey in literature books, I am excited to see the world’s diverse cultures, understand societies in different historical periods and absorb the humane messages of prestigious writers. They inspire and urge me to act for a better world. Meanwhile, scientific books satisfy my unquenchable thirst for knowledge. They blow my mind and excite me with answers to questions that I’ve been asking about the world.

Random fact: I used to open a service selling my old books with a friend in grade 4. Sometimes we wrote a few stories and sold them as well. The profits was really fine and I could afford for around 10 new books with them.

- EMP NTH -

Organized EMP class teaching robotics and programming languages for 35 students, wrote and managed 1000 followers page

EMP is the first school club I participated in at Nguyen Thuong Hien, and therefore, it always has a special meaning to me. EMP is where I’ve nurtured my passion for STEM through various activities, from researching about technology for posts on-page, managing EMP class to working with experienced alumni in the area to improve teaching materials. The Arduino and sensors that I’ve worked on, the experience that I’ve received from alumni about robotics, AI, and web development, and the opportunities to collaborate with other passionate students always inspire me to pursue my goals in computer science.

- Cactus Learners -

“Cactus Learners” is a website I’ve been developing from scratch to research and write about my favorite topics: Technology, Environment, and Astronomy. This is not only an environment for me to apply the front-end web development skills I’ve self-learned through courses on LinkedIn, Coursera, and EdX but also a place where I can indulge in my passion for those three topics and write about all intersting facts I read on both Vietnamese and foreign forums.

Random fact: Sometimes, I post some articles I wrote on Facebook groups, and once, I got over 2,300 reactions and 200 comments !!!

- HACKATHONS-

Participating in national Hackathons is my next step on my way pursuing computer science. There, I had the opportunities to match teams and work with experienced competitors, from whom I learned a lot of useful knowledge. The majority of competitors were university students and some were working in the STEM profession, which made the contest highly competitive and stressful. Working with such talented people enabled me to broaden my horizons about the necessary skills to become a professional computer scientist.

- Github projects -

Github is where I store random projects I made after wandering on Youtube tutorials and Stackoverflow: from simple games, a calculator, a random happy-birthday project to a whole website. Although some small games I've made haven't been completed yet, the process of recreating the games I used to play as a child, making my own website to indulge in, or doing something special for my beloved ones by utilizing the magical power of technology all fill me with excitement and pride. I hope I can soon bring out the more perfect versions of them. :>
