Interests

"Solitude" is how I summarise my interests. To a typical introvert like me, time alone is not only a special occasion to relax after stressful work but also my unique space to contemplate and explore my own inner "world" with special values

- BOOKS -

Books have been my best friends since I started learning the very first letters. In the beginning, I only considered books as a way to entertain myself in the absence of other high-tech devices. However, gradually, books became an irreplaceable part of my day. Through my journey in literature books, I am excited to see the world’s diverse cultures, understand societies in different historical periods, and absorb the humane messages of prestigious writers. They inspire and urge me to act for a better world. Meanwhile, scientific books satisfy my unquenchable thirst for knowledge. They blow my mind and excite me with answers to questions that I’ve been asking about the world.

Random fact: I used to open a service selling my old books with a friend in grade 4. Sometimes we wrote a few stories and sold them as well. The profits was really fine and I could afford for around 10 new books with them.

- MUSIC -

"夢は涙の先 雨風に負けず信じてる 晴れた空に　祈り届くまで" - Shonichi

"어쩌면 그 모든 아픔을 내딛고서라도 짧게 빛을 내볼까 봐 포기할 수가 없어" - To my youth

Different languages, but they all convey the same message: success will come after trials and errors. How wonderful! Music breaks the barriers among different cultures and enables us to connect, empathize, and be empathized with others, to heal and be healed by the melodies. That's why I love music, love to play my organ, hum a random tune appearing in my head, search for songs in foreign youtube channels to feel understood by "the unknown".

- PLANTS -

My grandmother has a very beautiful garden and she spent half of her every day taking care of it. And I'm proud to be "the heir" of that small garden. Therefore, I've prepared for such property since I was going to kindergarten. I have to say that observing plants grow up gradually is very satisfying.

Random fact: One of the most wonderful achievements I got at 8 was growing enough azuki beans for my grandmother's sweet soup.

Experiences

Starting roaming in every existing mathematics forum at the age of six, I have always been an enthusiast for STEM. The STEM subjects excite me with their appealing complexity and the arts of creating.

- EMP NTH -

Organized EMP class teaching robotics and programming languages for 35 students, wrote and managed 1000 followers page

EMP was the first school club I participated in at Nguyen Thuong Hien. Therefore, it always has a special meaning to me. EMP was where I nurtured my passion for STEM through various activities, from researching about technology for posts on-page, managing EMP class to working with experienced alumni in the area to improve teaching materials. The Arduino and sensors that I worked on, the experience that I received from alumni about robotics, AI, and web development, and the opportunities to collaborate with other passionate students all inspired me to pursue my goals in computer science.

- Cactus Learners -

“Cactus Learners” is a website I’ve been developing from scratch to research and write about my favorite topics: Technology, Environment, and Astronomy. This is not only an environment for me to apply the front-end web development skills I’ve self-learned through courses on LinkedIn, Coursera, and EdX but also a place where I can indulge in my passion for those three topics and write about all the interesting facts I read on both Vietnamese and foreign forums.

Random fact: Sometimes, I post some articles I wrote on Facebook groups, and once, I got over 2,300 reactions and 200 comments !!!
